SSL attacks target secure HTTPS connections.

Common SSL-related attacks:
- SSL stripping
- Man-in-the-Middle (MITM)
- Certificate spoofing
- Expired certificate exploitation

SSL stripping forces a connection from HTTPS to HTTP.
Attackers intercept traffic between users and legitimate servers.

Indicators:
- Browser warnings
- Invalid certificates
- Connection not secure message

Always verify HTTPS certificate authenticity.
